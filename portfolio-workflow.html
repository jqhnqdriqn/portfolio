05: Content Operations

Multi-Site
Content Workflow System

Designed and managed a Notion-based content operations workflow for 8 client websites bringing consistency, accountability, and quality control to a high-volume publishing operation.

Featured image placeholder add portfolio-workflow.jpg to assets/images/

The Problem

Managing content for 8 client websites simultaneously without a proper system is chaotic. Deadlines get missed, quality varies from site to site, writers don't know what's expected, and nothing gets properly reviewed before going live. That's the situation I walked into at PuttyWorks.

The Solution

I built a centralized Notion workspace that handled the full content lifecycle for all 8 clients from brief creation to publishing sign-off. Every stage had a clear owner, a clear status, and a clear definition of done.

System Components

  • Master Content Calendar: A single database with all articles across all 8 clients, filterable by site, status, writer, and due date
  • Brief Templates: Standardized brief format with keyword target, target audience, word count, reference URLs, and tone guidance one per client
  • Writer Assignments: Each brief linked directly to a writer with status tracking from assigned through to approved
  • QC Checklist: Every piece of content passed through a 12-point checklist before publishing covering SEO, formatting, factual accuracy, and brand voice
  • Publishing Tracker: Post-publish log with live URL, publish date, Surfer SEO score, and any post-publish issues flagged
  • Performance Notes: Monthly review section per client where rankings, traffic changes, and content gaps were logged

Results

  • Publishing consistency improved from ad hoc to scheduled across all 8 sites
  • Content rejection rate dropped significantly once brief standards were applied
  • Onboarding new writers went from 1-2 days of back-and-forth to a single Notion page
  • All clients had full visibility into their content pipeline at any time

Project Details

  • Agency: PuttyWorks Inc.
  • Sites Managed: 8
  • Period: 2024–2025

System Tools

  • Notion
  • Google Docs
  • Google Sheets
  • Surfer SEO
  • WordPress
