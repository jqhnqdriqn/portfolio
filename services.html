Services

What I Can
Do for You

From writing to technical fixes to full content operations. I handle the work so your website stays healthy and your content keeps performing.

01

Content Writing and Copywriting

SEO-driven content that reads like a real person wrote it. Whether it's a long-form blog post or a short service page, the goal is always clear, useful, and built to rank.

Long-form blog and article writing Landing and service page copy Location page writing Product page copy UX microcopy AI content editing and humanization Content brief creation
02

On-Page SEO Optimization

Making sure every page is set up correctly for search. Keyword placement, metadata, heading structure, internal links. The fundamentals that most people treat as optional but that actually drive rankings.

Keyword research (Ahrefs, SEMrush, SpyFu) Keyword mapping and planning Title tags and meta descriptions Heading structure optimization Internal linking strategy Orphan page identification and fixing Search intent alignment Surfer SEO content optimization
03

Technical SEO

The behind-the-scenes issues that quietly hurt your site's performance. Broken links, unoptimized images, missing schema, crawl errors. I find them, document them, and fix them.

Image optimization (size, format, filenames, alt tags) Broken link detection and fixes (Screaming Frog) SEO plugin setup (Yoast, Rank Math) Google Search Console monitoring Schema markup (JSON-LD) XML sitemap setup and management Crawl error resolution
04

WordPress Publishing and Formatting

For teams that just need posts published properly. Clean formatting, correct structure, SEO fields filled in, images optimized and placed. No chasing, no re-uploads.

Clean WordPress publishing Post formatting and visual structure Category, tag, and taxonomy setup SEO plugin field completion Featured image setup and optimization Scheduling and publishing management
05

Content Operations and Workflow

Managing the moving parts of a content program across one or several sites. Audits, quality control, editorial calendars, writer coordination. The operational layer that keeps everything consistent.

Content audits and quality control Multi-site publishing coordination Notion, Docs, and Sheets workflow setup Editorial calendar management Writer coordination and feedback Performance tracking and reporting
06

Visual Content and Page Editing

Pages that look clean, load properly, and present content the way it was meant to be read. Layout adjustments, image creation, responsive formatting, and visual consistency across the site.

Elementor page editing and layout Responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile) Canva blog image creation Visual consistency across pages CTA placement and formatting
