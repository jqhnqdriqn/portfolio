Hi, I'm John

SEO Specialist and Content Operator

I'm an SEO content specialist based in Taguig City, Philippines. Most of my work involves making sure websites stay clean, consistent, and properly optimized. Not the flashy strategy side, but the hands-on execution that actually keeps things running.

Over the past two years I've worked across 15 client websites in industries like construction, B2B SaaS, rental management, and real estate. I've published over 900 pieces of content and optimized 200+ pages using Surfer SEO.

What I care most about is reliability. If something is broken, I find it. If something is inconsistent, I fix it. I'm the kind of person who reads every meta description and checks every internal link because the small things matter more than people think.
