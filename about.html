Hi, I'm John

SEO Content Specialist & Website Operations

I'm an SEO content specialist who focuses on keeping websites clean, consistent, and fully optimized. Based in Taguig City, Philippines, I work with clients across multiple industries — from construction and B2B SaaS to rental management and real estate.

Most websites don't fail because of strategy. They fail because small issues get ignored. Broken links, missing alt tags, unoptimized images, inconsistent metadata. These problems build up over time and affect both rankings and user experience. This is the kind of work I handle every day.

With 2+ years of experience, I've published 900+ pieces of content, optimized 200+ pages via Surfer SEO, and coordinated content operations across 15 client websites.
