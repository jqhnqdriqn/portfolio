01: SEO Content Strategy

Builtfront.com
3-Month SEO Growth

Scaled a B2B construction platform from near-zero visibility to measurable organic growth in 90 days through content strategy, technical housekeeping, and zero manual outreach.

Project Overview

The objective was to scale search visibility and domain authority for Builtfront.com, a B2B platform serving the construction industry, within a 90-day window. The strategy focused on high-quality content production and technical site health to drive organic results no paid ads, no link-building outreach.

Key Strategies

The growth was achieved through a content-led approach with zero manual outreach. Every result came from the quality of the content itself and the structure supporting it.

  • Content Humanization: Refined all drafts to ensure a natural, professional tone that improved brand consistency and user engagement signals.
  • Pillar and Cluster Architecture: Restructured the site using comprehensive pillar guides and internal cluster content to establish deep topical authority across key construction topics.
  • Organic Authority Building: Scaled the Domain Rating from 2 to 11 purely through high-value content that naturally earned authority without an outreach campaign.
  • SEO Housekeeping: Conducted technical audits to fix broken links and optimize image metadata, ensuring maximum crawl efficiency throughout the content ramp-up.

Conclusion

By prioritizing link-worthy content and technical excellence, the domain achieved a 20x increase in search visibility and a major authority boost in just three months. This project demonstrated that consistent, well-structured content with proper technical foundations is enough to generate real organic growth even on a very young domain.
