02: SEO Content Writing

Hair Restoration Clinic
Service and Location Pages

Wrote SEO-optimized service and procedure pages for a hair restoration clinic, targeting procedure-specific and geographic search intent across key markets.

Featured image placeholder
Add portfolio-maxim.jpg to assets/images/

Project Overview

The client needed a set of location-specific and procedure-specific service pages that could rank for targeted search queries in competitive healthcare and cosmetic procedure markets. The work involved understanding complex medical procedures and translating them into clear, accessible, and search-optimized content.

The Challenge

Medical service pages require a delicate balance they need to be accurate and trustworthy while also being optimized for search and easy for patients to understand. Generic content doesn't cut it in this vertical. Each page had to speak to a specific audience, in a specific location, looking for a specific procedure.

Approach

  • Conducted keyword and competitor research for each procedure and location pairing
  • Wrote individual pages targeting distinct search intents (procedure-specific vs. location-specific)
  • Structured each page using E-E-A-T principles to support credibility signals
  • Optimized metadata, heading structure, and internal linking across all pages
  • Maintained consistent tone, brand voice, and medical accuracy throughout
