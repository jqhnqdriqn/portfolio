03 — Product Page Optimization

Heworitz.eu
Agricultural Product Page

Optimized a product category page for a European agricultural equipment brand — improving structure, copy clarity, and on-page SEO for a niche B2B market.

Featured image placeholder
Add portfolio-heworitz.jpg to assets/images/

Project Overview

Heworitz.eu is a European brand selling specialized agricultural equipment — including potato hillers and related tools. The product category pages needed better structure, clearer copy, and on-page SEO improvements to serve both organic search and users arriving from other channels.

The Work

  • Analyzed the existing product page structure and identified gaps in heading hierarchy and keyword coverage
  • Rewrote product descriptions to balance technical accuracy with readability for non-specialist buyers
  • Optimized title tags, meta descriptions, and alt text for primary agricultural search terms
  • Improved internal linking between related product categories
  • Ensured all copy reflected the brand's European market positioning and tone
